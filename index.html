How it works
1. Your text is split into chunks
2. GPT-4o mini reads each chunk and returns an emotion label + voice instruction
3. The instruction is passed to GPT-4o mini TTS at generation
4. Audio comes back with matching emotional tone

Green highlights show the voice instructions that will be applied. Toggle Voice+ on, then hit Parse Emotion to preview before generating.
