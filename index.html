How it works
1. Toggle Emotion Parse on and choose granularity
2. Optionally enable Multi-Voice and pick a mode
3. Paste text on the Read tab
4. Hit ✦ Parse Emotion or ◆ Parse Voice
5. Coloured tags appear per segment
6. Hit Generate — each segment gets its own TTS call
