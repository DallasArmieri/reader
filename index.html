How it works
1. Toggle Emotion Parse on and choose granularity
2. Optionally enable Multi-Voice and pick a mode
3. Paste text on the Read tab
4. Hit ✦ Parse Emotion above the text box
5. Green tags appear showing detected emotion per segment
6. Hit Generate — each segment gets its own expressive TTS call
